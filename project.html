Project

Our Interactive webmap of pokhara is designed to provide traveller with a one-stop destination for planning their dream vacations. Pokhara,one of the finest touristic location in world .Its beauty are praised by those landed here once.Considering its popularity in the world ,this web portal is designed.This interactive web portal is first of its type in pokhara providing traveller detail about the best location as per their interest and plan their time table in accordance. Our web portal is organized into different sections, including Popular places and Route finder . Each section is carefully curated to provide comprehensive and up-to-date information that will help travelers make informed decisions about their travel plans. We cover a wide range of destinations, from bustling lakeside areas to scenic mountains top. Our content includes detailed descriptions, high-quality images, maps, and other relevant information to help travelers visualize their trip and plan accordingly.
